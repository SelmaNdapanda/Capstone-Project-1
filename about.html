PYLADIES NAMIBIA TECH WORKSHOP 2023

​​​​​​The workshop is geared toward Namibian women in tech, students and school girls looking to acquire and practice new tech skills. With the support of motivated and inspiring university students and industrial proffessional ladies, girls will learn fundamental programming skills, soft skills and get a chance to apply them to real life projects.
