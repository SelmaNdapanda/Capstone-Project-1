"Hello! Sharing World"

PYLADIES NAMIBIA TECH WORKSHOP 2023

​​​​​​The workshop is geared toward Namibian women in tech, students and school girls looking to acquire and practice new tech skills. With the support of motivated and inspiring university students and industrial proffessional ladies, girls will learn fundamental programming skills, soft skills and get a chance to apply them to real life projects.

2023.06.16(FRI) ~ 18(MON)

@ Safari Court Hotel and Conference Centre, Windhoek, Namibia

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to speakers from various institutions and companies about the messages of sharing and opening.

Web Design

Focus on website building, Design, Publication and everything else you need to launch your own website.

Mobile App

Ladies will design, build and program their own mobile app

Discussion

Discussions with feature leading technology experts and professionals from all over the country.

Community

Introduction and Registration to join the communities and technology groups from across the country by experts.
